Frequently asked questions

Can we sign an NDA?
Yes, that would be the first step. We value your trust, business, and ideas. Your sensitive information will be handled with utmost confidentiality as we partner in your success.
Do you offer any guarantees?
Yes, all our plans come up with guaranteed story points for each month. And, we make sure your product is clean, bug-free and built with the best quality standards.
Do you provide support services after the product development completes?
Yes, we can provide you tailor made maintenance plans. And if needed, we can help you further develop the product with new features or integrating third party services.
Can you work across different time zones?
Our schedule aligns with yours, guaranteeing at least 3 hours of overlap to ensure there’s no delay in communication and we’re well-within your reach when needed.
Do you have experience working with in-house dev or design teams?
Yes, we have the experience and we love to collaborate with external teams. We believe working together eliminates clutter, brings more focus & clarity towards the big picture.
Do you employ freelancers?
No, we don’t employ freelancers. We have a handpicked expert team who have a proven track record of building quality products and go through continuous upskilling.
How do you certify the quality of features built?
We use automation tools to prioritize performance, security, and quality to ensure our standards are met. With our focus on automation, we deliver the best results.
What do you refer to as pre-built frameworks?
We turn many common functionalities and user interfaces into reusable components that can accelerate your product engineering efforts.
Who owns the code and IP?
You own 100% of the code and the IP except for our pre-built frameworks which are packaged as part of your code but we have the right to reuse them.
What industries do you offer?
We have worked with a wide variety of industries from travel, healthcare, fintech, collaboration, real-estate, eCommerce, web3, fan engagement to saas and much more.
What communication and project management tools do you use?
We use project management tools like Jira and Slack for communication. Reaching us shouldn't be a problem as we take full ownership of working on your product.
Where are your company and team located?
Our headquarters and our team are located in Chennai (SaaS capital of India), the bustling city is home to a thriving tech community, startups and established companies.
